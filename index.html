What is ChainSync?

A coordination and orchestration engine for emergencies, maintenance, and ESG compliance across smart industries.

Features

Predictive, Not Reactive

Our platform uses machine learning to identify and respond to potential threats before they escalate.

Automated Coordination

It instantly notifies agencies like the EPA and state DEQs, eliminating the need for manual reporting and reducing the risk of a misstep.

Cross-System Integration

It connects all your systems from water treatment to waste management, breaking down information silos and giving you a complete picture.

Scalable Architecture

Built on MuleSoft's enterprise-grade technology, ChainSync is reliable and powerful enough for mission-critical operations.
