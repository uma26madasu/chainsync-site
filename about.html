We're on a Mission

To transform emergency response from a reactive, manual process into a proactive, intelligent system.

Our Journey So Far

2022: The Idea

Our co-founders, former environmental regulators and software engineers, conceived of a platform that could automate response to environmental emergencies.

2023: MVP & Pilot Program

We built the first version of the ChainSync platform and partnered with a major metropolitan water utility for our first pilot program.

2024: Public Launch

After a successful pilot, we launched ChainSync to the public, focusing on water, waste, and remediation services.

2025: Expanding the Platform

We're adding new modules for supply chain logistics and energy, bringing intelligent orchestration to more critical sectors.
